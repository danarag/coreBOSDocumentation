====== GenDoc: Advanced OpenOffice Merge. ======

This extension permits creating advanced templates using [[https://www.openoffice.org/|OpenOffice]] or [[https://www.libreoffice.org/Libre Office]] which can easily be merged with fields in the coreBOS application and then converted to PDF or used as working documents in the word processor.
