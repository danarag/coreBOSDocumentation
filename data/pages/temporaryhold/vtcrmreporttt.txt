====== A trouble ticket report in vtiger CRM ======

**Trouble Tickets within a week**\\

This report should detail a list of all clients who have trouble tickets in an OPEN status with a category of Big Problem and have been open for over 7 days. The 7 days period should be counted back from the date the report is run.\\
